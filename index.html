03 ·  How It Works

Brief in.
Ad out.

One studio. Three steps. No producers, no decks, no pass-the-baton between five vendors. You write the brief; you get the master back.

/ 01   BRIEF

Lock the angle.

You bring the product, the goal, and the audience. I bring the angle, the storyboards, and the shot list — usually inside a week.

  • Concept & mood
  • Storyboard / animatic
  • Aspect-ratio plan
/ 02   BUILD

Model. Light. Direct.

Modeled in Blender, shaded for camera, simulated where it matters, lit like a film set. Every frame is a directed shot — not a render dump.

  • Modeling & shading
  • Sim & FX (EmberGen)
  • Cinematic lighting
/ 03   SHIP

Cut, grade, master.

Comp in After Effects, grade in DaVinci, packaged for every platform you need. Hero spot, vertical cuts, square cuts — all from the same shoot.

  • Comp & finish
  • Color grade
  • Master + cutdowns
